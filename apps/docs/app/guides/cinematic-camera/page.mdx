# Cinematic Camera

Tokovo camera work should emphasize beats, not simulate constant arbitrary motion.

## Recommended Pattern

- use semantic anchors
- use long tracking spans for sustained attention
- use short explicit focus or shake beats for interruptions and reveals

## Use Automatic Direction When

- the scene has repetitive conversation coverage
- consistency matters more than shot-by-shot uniqueness

## Use Manual Camera When

- the scene has a custom dramatic rhythm
- the framing itself is part of the storytelling
