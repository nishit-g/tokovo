# Flirty WhatsApp Romance - Episode Script

**Title:** "The Game of Flirts"
**Characters:** Aarav (28, techie) & Ananya (26, designer)
**Setting:** Late evening, they match on a dating app and move to WhatsApp

---

## SCENE 1: The First Message

**[Chat: Aarav → Ananya]**

**Aarav:** So... I swiped right because of your dog's picture. Just to be clear. 🐕
**[Delivered]**

**Ananya:** 😂 LOL. Fair enough. Sir Whiskers demands an explanation though - he says he's more than just a wingman

**Aarav:** Sir Whiskers sounds like a man of culture. I'll have you know I also appreciate fine coffee, bad puns, and apparently very cute girls with even cuter dogs 👀

**Ananya:** Wow. Smooth. Did you practice that in the mirror? 🪞

**Aarav:** Maybe once or twice... or five times. Don't judge me.

**Ananya:** 😏 I'm not judging. I'm... mildly impressed.

---

## SCENE 2: Voice Note Exchange

**[Voice Note from Aarav - 0:23]**
*"Okay so I was thinking... you seem cool, you have a cute dog, and you're apparently a designer which means you have taste. So... coffee sometime? I promise I'll try to be less awkward in person."*

**[Voice Note from Ananya - 0:18]**
*"Awwww that was adorable. And yes, I'm holding you to that 'less awkward' promise. But also... kind of hoping you stay a little awkward. It's cute."* 🎧

**Aarav:** 👀 You're dangerous, you know that?

**Ananya:** Dangerous? I'm literally sitting on my couch in oversized pajamas right now 😭

**Aarav:** Okay NOW I'm imagining that and it's doing things to my brain. Stop.

**Ananya:** 😂 Make me.

---

## SCENE 3: Late Night Banter

**[11:47 PM]**

**Aarav:** Okay but real talk - why are you still awake

**Ananya:** Because my brain won't stop thinking about something... 💭

**Aarav:** Oh? Do I want to know 👀

**Ananya:** Maybe... what you look like in those pajamas 👀👀

**Aarav:** Ananya. You're playing with fire here 🔥

**Ananya:** Someone had to make the first move 😏

**Aarav:** Bold of you to assume I haven't already imagined like 47 different scenarios of us meeting

**Ananya:** Forty seven?? That's very specific 🙄

**Aarav:** I've had time. Lots of time. Too much time actually. Send help.

**Ananya:** 😂 No help coming from this side. I enjoy the chaos.

---

## SCENE 4: The Sweet Turn

**Aarav:** Okay real talk for a sec though...

**Aarav:** Like genuinely - I had the worst week and somehow talking to you makes it feel... lighter? That's weird right? We've only been talking for like two days 😂

**Ananya:** 🥺 Not weird at all. That's kind of the whole point, isn't it? Finding someone who makes the bad days feel less heavy.

**Aarav:** When did this become deep 😂💕

**Ananya:** Around the same time you started using the heart emoji 😏

**Aarav:** I mean... it was relevant 🤷

**Ananya:** Sure it was. 😉

**Aarav:** I KNEW you'd tease me about that

**Ananya:** It's what I do best. Get used to it. 😘

---

## SCENE 5: Planning the Date

**Aarav:** Okay so hypothetically... if I asked you out for Saturday... what would hypothetically be your answer

**Ananya:** Hypothetically? 👀

**Aarav:** YES hypothetically

**Ananya:** Hypothetically... I'd say yes. BUT with conditions.

**Aarav:** I'm listening 👂

**Ananya:** 1) You have to be at least as charming as your texts
**Ananya:** 2) Coffee is MANDATORY
**Ananya:** 3) Sir Whiskers gets a cameo at some point

**Aarav:** Those are VERY reasonable conditions. I accept.

**Ananya:** Wow. That was fast. You were waiting for that weren't you 😏

**Aarav:** Maybe.

**Ananya:** Thought so. 💕

**Aarav:** ...so that's a real yes then? For Saturday?

**Ananya:** Aarav. Yes. It's a real yes. Now please go to sleep it's 1am 😭

**Aarav:** Only because YOU said so 💫

**Ananya:** 🔥 Oh he's gonna be trouble. Goodnight, smooth talker.

**Aarav:** Goodnight, Ananya. 🌙

---

**[Typing indicator shows Aarav is typing...]**

**Aarav:** (one more thing)

**Aarav:** I really can't wait to meet you. Like... really. 💖

**[Seen 1:03 AM]**

**Ananya:** 💕 Me too.

---

## END SCENE

- **Emojis:** Should animate/float gently for visual appeal
- **Voice:** Aarav's voice should be warm, slightly nervous; Ananya's should be playful, confident
- **Duration:** ~3-4 minutes of dialogue for video episode
- **Tone:** Light, romantic, warm - perfect for cozy viewing
