# Code Of Conduct

Tokovo contributors are expected to keep project spaces focused, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and technical.
- Assume good intent, but correct problems clearly.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping issues and pull requests scoped.
- Discuss ideas and implementation tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Give credit for work, reports, designs, and prior art.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or targeted insults.
- Sexualized, discriminatory, or otherwise hostile conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for it to stop.
- Bad-faith reports, spam, or attempts to bypass project security processes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or restrict participation when behavior damages the project or community.

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer. If the concern involves a maintainer, contact another maintainer when available.
