# Movement Recipes

## Focus on Message
- Use `focus("lastMessage")` (WhatsApp) or `focus("tweet_card")` (X) with a small scale bump (1.05–1.2).
- Use short durations (0.3–0.6s) for natural snap.

## Slow Push-In
- Start at scale 1.0, animate to 1.1–1.2 over 2–4s.

## Cut + Reframe
- Use `set` for instant cuts, then `animate` for micro-adjusts.

## Anchor Transitions
- Move between anchors with consistent scale; avoid sudden zoom jumps.
