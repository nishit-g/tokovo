# Timing and Frames

## Time Inputs
- Numbers are frames.
- Strings support `ms` and `s` via `parseTimeToFrames`.

## Tips
- Use consistent FPS for conversions.
- Prefer frames for tight timing.
- Avoid mixing seconds and frames in the same sequence.

## Repo Paths
- Time utils: `packages/dsl/src/v2/utils/time.ts`
